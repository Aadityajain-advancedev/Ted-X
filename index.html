01 — Event Overview

Where Ideas
Ignite Change.

TEDx TIET is an independently organized TED event hosted at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, Patiala. We bring together the most compelling voices — thinkers, innovators, artists, and changemakers — to share ideas that challenge, inspire, and transform the way we see the world.

From technology to philosophy, from science to social impact, our speakers traverse disciplines and break boundaries. This is not just a conference — it is an intellectual awakening, a celebration of human curiosity, and a platform where the next generation of ideas takes flight.

02 — About Us

Our Story

← Scroll to explore →

01

Who We Are

We are a student-led organization at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, united by a belief in the power of ideas to reshape the world. TEDx TIET is powered by passionate individuals from every discipline — engineers, artists, entrepreneurs — who believe that one conversation can change everything.

Our team handles everything from speaker curation and production to design and community engagement, ensuring every event is an experience that stays with you long after the lights go down.

02

Our Mission

To create a platform where ideas — however unconventional, however audacious — are given the space and audience they deserve. We are committed to democratizing access to world-class thought leadership and fostering a culture of curiosity, empathy, and open dialogue on campus and beyond.

We exist to challenge comfortable thinking, bridge communities, and inspire action — one talk at a time.

03

Our Vision

To make TEDx TIET the most anticipated intellectual event in the region — a nucleus where ideas from the campus radiate outward and leave a measurable impact on the world. We envision a future where every student leaves our event believing they have something worth saying, and the courage to say it.

Ideas do not live in isolation. They grow when shared. We are building the ground for that growth.

AR

Dr. Ananya Rajan

Neuroscientist & Author

Talk: "The Conscious Paradox"

Dr. Ananya Rajan is a leading neuroscientist whose research on memory, identity, and consciousness has been published in over 40 peer-reviewed journals. Her TEDx TIET talk challenged the audience to reconsider what we mean by "free will" in an age of algorithmic decision-making. Warm, witty, and profoundly erudite, her talk remains one of our most-viewed.

RM

Rohan Mehta

Climate Entrepreneur

Talk: "Soil, Soul, and Systems Change"

Rohan Mehta left a promising career in finance to found a network of regenerative farms across Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh. His work has impacted over 12,000 farming families. At TEDx TIET, he made a compelling case for why the next generation of entrepreneurs must look to the earth — not just the cloud — for solutions to the climate crisis.

Watch Talk →
PS

Priya Sundaram

Digital Rights Activist

Talk: "Reclaiming the Mind Online"

Priya Sundaram is a lawyer, technologist, and co-founder of a leading digital rights organization based in Bengaluru. Her work focuses on data sovereignty, algorithmic accountability, and the commodification of human attention. At TEDx TIET, she delivered a sharp, urgent talk that left the audience rethinking every app on their phones.
